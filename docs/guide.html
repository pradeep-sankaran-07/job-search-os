The Job Search OS

in Claude Code

One 15-minute setup. After that, Claude orchestrates your job search every day — you spend minutes reviewing what lands, not hours on the grunt work. And because it knows your CV, your profile, and your targets, you can ask it for anything: a tailored CV, a re-pitched cover letter, a referral note.

The problem

The grunt work crowds out the thinking.

Time pressure makes you cut corners. Generic cover letters. Roles you miss. Applications to jobs already filled. The thinking that actually lands interviews gets squeezed out.

What it does

Claude as your on-standby agent.

Runs the search, writes the letters, tracks the roles. Because it knows everything about you, just ask — a tailored CV, a custom pitch, a LinkedIn intro, whatever you need.

Install
  1. Install Claude Code — docs.claude.com/en/docs/claude-code
  2. Make sure you’re on Claude Pro or Max (required to use Claude Code)
  3. Open Claude Code and paste this message: “Can you please install the plugin from github.com/pradeep-sankaran-07/job-search-os and walk me through the setup?”
Needs Claude (Pro or Max) and Chrome browser installed. Works on macOS, Linux, and Windows — the setup auto-detects your OS.
What actually happens
  • Creates a local folder on your machine for all your job-search files.
  • Every morning it searches Finn, LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Google, and your target companies’ career pages.
  • Fills an Excel tracker: each role gets a short summary, a fit rating (Strong / Medium), and a direct link.
  • Drafts a personalized cover letter for every Strong match — in your voice.
  • You open the Excel and update the Status column: Apply, Will not apply, or Not a real job. Claude learns from your choices.
  • On demand: ask Claude for a tailored CV, a tweak to a letter, a LinkedIn outreach — it knows you.
  • Found a job yourself? Drop the screenshot into Claude. It adds it to the tracker, writes a letter, rates the fit.
Three steps, all in plain English
1

Drop your CV + build your profile

Claude reads your CV, asks short questions about what you’re looking for (titles, locations, seniority, dealbreakers), and gives you a polished CV back along with a saved profile.

“Here’s my CV — let’s set up my job search. Polish it if needed, then ask me what you need to know.”
2

Pick target companies

Claude Code sends you to Claude chat (claude.ai) for this — it has a stronger Research mode.

“Let’s figure out my target companies.”
  1. In Claude chat, turn on Research mode.
  2. Paste the prompt Claude Code gives you (your profile is already included).
  3. Save the list as target-companies.md in your folder and come back.
3

Your daily run

Every morning Claude updates your Excel tracker in your folder. Strong-fit cover letters are pre-drafted. You open the Excel and update the Status column — Apply, Will not apply, or Not a real job.

“What’s new today?”
