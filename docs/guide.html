“Here’s my CV — let’s set up my job search. Polish it if needed, then ask me what you need to know.”
2

Pick target companies

For this one step, switch to Chat mode in the Claude desktop app — Chat has a stronger Research mode for multi-source web research.

  1. Switch to Chat mode in the Claude desktop app.
  2. Turn on Research mode (toggle near the chat input).
  3. Paste the prompt Claude Code gave you (your profile is already included).
  4. Save the list as target-companies in your folder, then switch back to Code mode.

What actually happens

Under the hood.

Once set up, Claude runs in the background every morning. Here’s what the plugin does on your behalf — so you know exactly what to expect and where to intervene.

Your local folder
When you install the plugin, it creates a folder on your machine for everything: your CV, profile, target-company list, tracker, cover letters, and logs. Nothing leaves your computer.
Daily search — scheduled
Every morning, Claude automatically searches LinkedIn, Indeed, Google, and the career pages of your target companies. It dedupes and verifies every match in a real browser so dead links never reach you.
Excel tracker, auto-filled
Each role shows up with a short summary, fit rating (Strong or Medium), direct link, and a Status column you control.
Cover letters, pre-drafted
For every Strong match, a personalized first-draft cover letter is written in your voice, ready in your folder. Tweak or ask Claude to rewrite — whichever is faster.
Your daily move
Open the Excel. Pick what to apply to. Mark the Status: Apply, Will not apply, or Not a real job (for false positives). Claude learns from every choice.
On demand
Ask Claude for a tailored CV for one role, a re-pitched cover letter, a LinkedIn intro, or anything else. It knows your CV, your profile, and your targets — it doesn’t need context.
Found one elsewhere?
Spotted a job opportunity somewhere outside the tracker — a tip from a friend, a LinkedIn post, an email? Drop the screenshot or link into Claude and ask it to process. It adds the role to the tracker, drafts a cover letter, and rates the fit — same flow as everything else.
