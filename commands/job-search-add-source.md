argument-hint: <source name> [--disable]

Invoke the `job-search-add-source` skill with the user's argument.

v0.1 supports: Finn, LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor. For anything else, the skill explains the v0.1 limitation and suggests `/job-search-add` (manual per-job entry) or waiting for v0.2's auto-detect cascade.
